Introduction

Welcome to Arzan . We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and topup services.

By using Arzan, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy. We specialize in providing mobile recharge services across multiple countries with a primary focus on Afghanistan operators. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide when you register or interact with our Services:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Account credentials and authentication information
  • Payment information and billing details
  • Mobile numbers for topup services
  • Geographic location data
  • Profile picture (if provided via Google Sign-In)

Authentication Information

We use Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication to secure your account. When you sign in, we may collect:

  • Your Google account name and email address
  • Profile picture (if available and authorized)
  • Authentication tokens for secure access

Usage Information

We automatically collect information about your use of our Services:

  • Transaction history and topup records
  • Device information (type, operating system, IP addresses)
  • App activity logs and usage patterns
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

  • • Process mobile topup transactions
  • • Communicate with mobile operators
  • • Send transaction confirmations
  • • Provide customer support

Account Management

  • • Create, maintain, and secure accounts
  • • Enable Google and Firebase authentication
  • • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • • Comply with legal requirements

Service Improvement

  • • Analyze usage patterns and optimize performance
  • • Develop new features and improve existing ones
  • • Monitor service performance and stability
  • • Personalize user experience

Communication

  • • Send important updates and security notifications
  • • Provide customer support responses
  • • Share promotional offers and service updates
  • • Respond to inquiries and feedback

Data Storage and Security

All user data, including authentication details, is securely stored in Google Firebase. We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

End-to-end encryption for data transmission and industry-standard protocols for data at rest.

Secure Infrastructure

Firebase implements robust security protocols with regular security updates and monitoring.

Access Control

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data.

Regular Security Updates

Continuous security monitoring and updates to keep your data safe from threats.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

Mobile Operators

We share necessary information with mobile network operators to process your topup requests, especially with Afghanistan operators and international partners.

Service Providers

We may share limited data with Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and with trusted payment processors.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court orders, or to detect and prevent fraud, cyber threats, or unauthorized activities.

Security Purposes

To protect our users' rights, safety, and the integrity of our services from fraudulent or harmful activities.

Your Rights and Data Deletion

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Correction

Access, update, or correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information and account data.

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for data collection and opt-out of marketing communications.

How to Delete Your Data

If you want to delete your Arzan account and associated data, you have two options:

1. In-App Deletion

  • • Open the Arzan App
  • • Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete My Account
  • • Confirm the deletion request

2. Manual Request

If you cannot delete your account through the App:

  • • Send an email to admin@arzantopup.com
  • • Use subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • • Include your registered email address or phone number
  • • We will process your request within 30 days

Data Retention Policy

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. If your account remains inactive for 24 months, we may delete your data automatically.

Exception for Legal Compliance

Certain data may be retained for legal, security, or compliance purposes, such as:

  • Transaction records for financial compliance
  • Fraud prevention records
  • Legal dispute resolution documentation
  • Regulatory reporting requirements

Children's Privacy

Arzan is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately and terminate the account.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and on this page, and we will update the "Last updated" date. If significant changes are made, we will notify users via email or in-app notifications. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
